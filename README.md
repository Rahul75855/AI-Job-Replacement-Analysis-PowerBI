# AI Job Replacement & Workforce Transformation Analysis using Power BI

## Overview
This project is a Power BI case study focused on analyzing the impact of Artificial Intelligence on jobs, industries, salaries, automation risk, workforce disruption, and future employment trends.

The dashboard provides insights into how AI adoption is reshaping industries across the world and identifies which job categories are most vulnerable or resistant to automation.



## Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze AI automation risk across industries
- Compare high-risk and low-risk jobs
- Study salary vs automation relationships
- Identify future growth opportunities in AI-related jobs
- Understand workforce disruption trends globally
- Provide strategic recommendations for students and employees

## Key Insights

- Higher-paying jobs generally show lower automation risk.
- Technical and AI-related jobs demonstrate stronger future growth.
- Non-technical repetitive jobs face higher automation exposure.
- AI adoption levels vary significantly across countries.
- Technology-driven industries continue creating new opportunities despite automation growth.
- Continuous reskilling and adaptability are becoming essential in the AI-driven economy.



## Strategic Recommendations

- Employees should continuously upskill themselves in AI, analytics, and digital technologies.
- Students should focus on technical, analytical, and problem-solving skills.
- Industries vulnerable to automation should invest in workforce reskilling programs.
- AI investment should prioritize human-AI collaboration rather than complete workforce replacement.



## Conclusion
This case study demonstrates that Artificial Intelligence is significantly transforming industries, workforce structures, and future job opportunities. While AI automation may replace repetitive and predictable tasks, it is also creating new opportunities in technology-driven and analytical fields.

The analysis highlights the growing importance of adaptability, continuous learning, and human-centric skills such as creativity, leadership, and critical thinking. Organizations, employees, and students must prepare for an AI-driven future through reskilling and strategic workforce transformation.



## Future Scope
This project can be further improved by integrating real-time labor market data, predictive analytics, and machine learning models to forecast workforce changes more accurately. Additional datasets related to economic growth, education, and AI investment trends can provide deeper business insights.
